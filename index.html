About Us

About Us

Founded in 2023 under Lions Clubs International, the Leo Club of Colombo Hogwarts operates as a non-profit organization with strong community values. Spearheaded by President Leo Akila Weragoda and Vice President Leo Lion Senula Deelana, and with guidance from esteemed figures like PDG Dr. Lion Lasantha Goonewardene and Leo Lion Binuja Amaranayaka, the club has swiftly grown to encompass 100 dedicated members aged 18 to 30. Embodying the theme "Together We Achieve Greatness," the club focuses on community engagement and empowerment. Their initiatives bridge urgent gaps in society, reflecting a resolute commitment to driving positive change.

Featured Projects

Ashirwada Poojawa 23'

Colombo Mid City, Leo District 306 C2 organized "ASHIRWADA POOJAWA 23," invoking blessings for a prosperous Leoistic year. Religious ceremonies were held at temples, a mosque, a church, and a Hindu kovil, symbolizing unity and cultural respect. The event marked Leo Club of Colombo Hogwarts' inaugural year, fostering a spirit of collaboration among members.

Youth Vision - Car Meetup

Leo Youth Sports Encounter and the car meetup: A collaboration between Leo Clubs of Royal, Thurstan, and Colombo Hogwarts with the National Youth Services Council, promoting sports spirit among students. Commenced on July 31, 2023, featuring 2000+ athletes from 23 Colombo schools. Dignitaries like Chief Guest HON Sagala Rathnayaka marked the vibrant inauguration.

Words from the Top Board

Lions International has greenlit the establishment of Leo Club of Colombo Hogwarts, a platform offering youth leadership experience through community activities. The Leo Moto, "Leadership, Experience, Opportunity," hones skills like project management and teamwork. Kudos to President Akila Weragoda and VP Senula Deelana for their efforts. The club aspires to serve and lead, forging enduring connections and nurturing leaders while impacting society.
